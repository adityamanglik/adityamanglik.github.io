Biography

Hi! I am a graduate student at ETH Zürich, advised by Prof. Onur Mutlu. I am broadly interested in systems, specializing in computer architecture and secure system design. Before joining ETH Zürich, I worked as a cloud engineer at Oracle Corporation.

I earned a Bachelor’s in Electrical Engineering (Hons.) from BITS Pilani and wrote a bachelor’s thesis at TU Wien (supervised by Prof. Muhammad Shafique and Prof. Surekha Bhanot). My prior research focuses on Non-Volatile Memory microarchitectures for accelerating deep learning workloads.
